---
title: Adding a New Project Documentation
description: Quick steps to add docs for a new project.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Projects">
    From the dashboard, click on "Projects" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Project">
    Click "New Project", enter your project name, and select a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Docs">
    Add sections and pages as needed, then hit "Publish" to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
